FAQ

1HOW TO REGISTER?

Follow the steps below:

  1. Click on "Register" in Homepage or on the top right corner
  2. Enter the registration details on the form
  3. After entering the details, click "Register". An Activation mail will be sent to the registered mail
  4. Login into your email-account and click the link sent from Gyanmitra'19
  5. Upon clicking the link, you will be redirected to the gyanmitra homepage and your account is registered. You can login and view/register for events and workshops
2I didn't receive confirmation mail/account activation mail?

Check your inbox/spam folder of your registered email address

3 I couldn't login.

Check your mail address and password and try again

4I forgot my password, What should I do?

Click " Forget password" and you will receive the steps to reset the password to your registered mail

5 What is the fee for event and workshops?

  • Rs.200 irrespective of number of events
  • The payment for the workshop is listed in the details of workshop
6 I Couldn't add a member to my team

It may be one of the reasons below

  • Team max limit reached
  • The member you are trying to add haven't register a gyanmitra account
  • The event may not allow gender mixing
7 Should every member for the team register for the event seperately?

No, Only the team leader must add the members during the registration of the event, all the members will be registered automatically.

8 Can I withdraw from an event/workshop?

Yes, If you haven't paid for the event/workshop, you can withdraw from the event/workshop you want to from the cart section.

9I have a doubt regarding the workshop/event.

No, Only the team leader must add the members during the registration of the event, all the members will be registered automatically.

10I have a doubt regarding the workshop/event.

Please send the mail to the contact mail address in the description of the workshop/event you need to clear the doubt

11How to request for accomodation?

You can apply for accomodation in the Accomodation section.

12 What is the maximum number of days I can apply for?

2 days

13 Can I come in the early morning/late night on or before the day of event?

Yes, Our hospitality team will receive you at the gate and show to the place you are accomodated.

14 My accomodation request is still pending?

Please wait patient till the admin approves your request. If the request is denied, you can see the status in accommodation section

15 Can I cancel my accommodation request?

Once the payment is done towards the accomodation in the cart section, your request for accomomdation on arrival will not be entertained.

16 How can I pay for the events/workshop/accomodation?

You can do the payment in the cart section through online payment or by sending the scanned copy of DD.

17 Is there on-spot registration/payment?

No, please register and pay either through online-payment or DD.

18 My payment through DD is not yet confirmed?

Please be patient till the admin validates and approves your DD.
